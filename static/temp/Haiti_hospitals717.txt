Please note: The Department of State assumes no responsibility or liability for the professional ability or reputation of, or the quality of services provided by, the entities or individuals whose names appear on the following lists. Inclusion on this list is in no way an endorsement by the Department or the U.S. government. Names are listed alphabetically, and the order in which they appear has no other significance. The information on the list is provided directly by the local service providers; the Department is not in a position to vouch for such information.
If you need emergency medical attention, please see our list ofHospitals in Haiti(PDF 152 KB).
If you need the assistance of a doctor for a non-emergency situation, please see the U.S. Embassys List of Medical Professionals in Haiti(PDF288 KB).If you need to be medically evacuated out of Haiti, you should contact a medevac company, an air ambulance service, or a charter airline. The Embassy maintains a List of Cholera Treatment Centers as well as aList of Air Ambulance Services(PDF 163 KB).
If you need further assistance, please contact the U.S. Embassy by calling +509 2229-8000, Monday through Friday from 7:00 a.m. to 3:30 p.m. After hours, please call +509 2229-8122.
NOTE: None of these lists is meant to be exhaustive or definitive, nor do they represent a guarantee of competence or endorsement by the Department of State or the American Embassy. These lists indicate that the American community has utilized these providers in the past. Retention on this list depends upon a combination of factors including availability, training, and positive assistance in previous consultations, etc. Patient input, both positive and negative, regarding experience with local health care providers is always welcome and provides additional information to assist in expanding or contracting the list.
