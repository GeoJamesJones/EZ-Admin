Ukraine




Ekol entered the Ukrainian market in 2012 and is currently one of the most vital logistics and contract logistics companies in Ukraine.
Ekol Ukraine has warehouses with a total capacity of 61,000 square meters; over 1,000 trailers, trucks, and other vehicles; and over 680 professional employees experienced in logistics. With the help of the said resources, Ekol Ukraine offers its customers excellent quality, tailor-made solutions in international road transportation, national delivery, warehousing, and customs clearance.

Ekol Ukraines Smart Solutions for Diverse Industries




As one of the most prominent logistics companies in the country, Ekol Ukraine offers integrated logistic services and supply chain solutions to its national and international customers from a variety of fields. These include the FMCG, textile, and manufacturing industries, all served by the companys high level of expertise, qualified human resources, and the incorporation of cutting-edge technology.




Ekol Ukraine Focus on Quality and Technology





Ekol Ukraine operates in line with the highest innovation and quality standards. Its in-house R&D team keeps up-to-date with the latest breakthroughs, and adapts new software to the Ukrainian market. Ekol Ukraine customers enjoy the advantages of leading-edge software and technology such as Rainbow, Quadro, and Quadronet, and Put-to-Light  the latter developed by its own R&D Department.
The company maintains all its operations in accordance with standards such as:

ISO 9001:2015 Quality Management Systems,
OHSAS 18001:2007 Occupational Health and Safety Management Systems, and
ISO 14001:2015 Environmental Management Systems.

The Integrated Management System (IMS) Policy you can find here, while the significant environmental aspects are the following:

Company vehicles utilization. Waste production (scrap tires, oil, accumulators)
Spent car batteries accumulation
Particular matters production (tape, cardboard) by goods admission, pallet tape/skid replacement
Skids scrapping
Oils leakage at the damaged equipment utilization, as well as by oil replacement






Green Logistics




Ekol Ukraine recognizes that protecting nature and the environment is one of its fundamental responsibilities. Ekol maintains its operations with an eco-friendly approach, and is constantly developing new technologies to reduce its environmental impact and protect natural resources.
