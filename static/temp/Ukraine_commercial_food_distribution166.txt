Partnership in Ukraine
TERRA FOOD  a leading company in dairy products on the Ukrainian market. We are always open to partnership and offer those in Ukraine cooperation in the following areas.

DISTRIBUTING THE COMPANY'S PRODUCTS
Today the TERRA FOOD company has an extensive distribution network, and thanks to it our products are represented in all regions of the country. We strive for the further effective development and invite distributors to cooperation.


   To find the contact information of the closest distributor to you can be done in theDistributorssection.  
DIRECT SALES
We are actively working with all large retail networks in Ukraine. Our products are available at the following supermarket chains: Silpo, ATB-Market, METRO Cash and Carry Ukraine, Furshet, Velyka Kyshenya and Alliance Market. We invite the retail-partners to cooperation.
 
 
PRODUCTION OF YOUR OWN BRANDED TRADE MARKS FOR RETAIL NETWORKS
One of the directions of our activities  is the production of your own trade marked products for retail networks. We successfully cooperate with leaders of Ukrainian retail, such as the Fozzy Group and METRO Cash and Carry Ukraine.
 
THE SALE OF INDUSTRIAL PRODUCTS TO PRODUCTION COMPANIES
The TERRA FOOD company proposes the industrial products to companies: technical acidic casein and dry demineralized whey.

Regarding questions and possibilities of partnership and for additional information please contact our commercial department at the phone numbers listed on our Contactpage.
