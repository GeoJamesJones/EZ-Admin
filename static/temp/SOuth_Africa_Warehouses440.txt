ALP WestNairobiALP is developing a modern grade-A industrial park on 49 acres of land situated in Tilisis Logistics Park, 25km West of Nairobi's CBD. The site benefits from direct access on the the main Nairobi-Nakuru highway which is being upgrade to six lanes. ALP has begun infrastructure development and earthworks on the site. The total development will comprise of 100,000 square metres of modern warehousing space. Phase 1 will comprise flexible smaller units with space of between 500 - 2,000 sqm. For larger occupiers, ALP can finance and deliver bespoke built-to-suit industrial facilities of 2,000 sqm upwards.
