U.S. allocates $4 million for construction of ammo warehouse in Ukraine
The Memorandum of understanding between Ukraines Defence Ministry and The Halo Trust NGO was signed on May 7, 2019 
12:04, 27 June 2019













depo.ua


The U.S. State Department allocated $4 million for implementation of the goals of Memorandum on the construction of warehouses on the stockpiles, bases and ammo storage warehouses of the Armed Forces of Ukraine, as Ukraines Defence Ministry reported.
On May 7, 2019 a Memorandum of understanding between Ukraines Defence Ministry and The Halo Trust NGO on the construction of warehouses on the stockpiles, bases and ammo storage warehouses of the Armed Forces of Ukraine was signed, the message reads.
Related:  Extra $18,950 allocated for audit of Ukroboronprom state defense concern
As it was reported, the implementation of the mentioned Memorandum will be performed through the donor  the U.S. State Department.
